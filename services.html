Work and stay well

Personal training — functional fitness

Horayma is a certified personal trainer specializing in functional fitness — a full-body approach that builds strength, mobility, and conditioning through multi-joint movements that transfer directly to real life and sport. Unlike traditional gym machines or bodybuilding, functional fitness works multiple muscle groups at once, making sessions time-efficient and highly adaptable to any fitness level. One-on-one sessions can be arranged at the on-site gym for guests of any property. Ask about availability and rates when booking.

Group fitness classes

Group functional fitness classes run regularly at the on-site studio. Guests are welcome to join — a great way to start the day and connect with others on the property.

Co-working day pass — Mini Casa Piri

Not staying overnight but need a quiet desk for the day? Ask about day-use access to the Mini Casa Piri workspace.

Explore

Guided MTB rides

Horayma is an active mountain bike athlete and knows the trails around Pirenópolis well. Guided rides through the cerrado — from beginner-friendly routes to more technical trails — can be arranged on request.

Guided waterfall tours

The area around Pirenópolis is home to some of Brazil's most beautiful waterfalls: Cachoeira do Lázaro, Cachoeira do Abade, Vale da Lua, and more. Guided half-day or full-day excursions can be organized on request.

Arrive smoothly

Airport transfer

Door-to-door transfers from Brasília International Airport (BSB, ~150 km) or Goiânia Airport (GYN, ~130 km) can be arranged in advance. Ideal for international arrivals with luggage.

Early / late check-in

Flexible check-in and check-out times available on request, subject to availability. Contact Horayma directly when booking.

Welcome basket

Arrive to a curated selection of local Goiás products — Café do Cerrado, regional snacks, and seasonal fruit. Available as an add-on at booking.
