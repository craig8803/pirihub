Our services

At PiriHub, we offer a comprehensive range of services to enhance your coliving experience and make daily life easier.

Housekeeping

Our professional housekeeping team maintains common areas, ensuring a clean and welcoming environment for all residents. Services include weekly cleaning of shared spaces, restocking of supplies, and deep cleaning on a monthly basis.

Maintenance

Need something fixed? Our on-site maintenance team is available to handle repairs, maintenance requests, and general upkeep. From plumbing to electrical issues, we've got you covered with quick response times.

Community events

We organize regular community events and activities to foster connection and friendship among residents. From movie nights and potluck dinners to outdoor adventures and cultural outings, there's always something happening at PiriHub.

Wifi and tech support

Reliable high-speed internet is essential for remote workers. We provide robust wifi coverage throughout all houses, backup connectivity, and dedicated tech support to ensure you stay connected.

Laundry service

Optional laundry services are available for residents who prefer not to wash their clothes themselves. Professional laundry handling keeps your clothes fresh and extends their lifespan.

Meal preparation

Our optional meal preparation service offers healthy, delicious meals prepared by local chefs. Choose from weekly meal plans or à la carte options, all made with fresh local ingredients.

Coworking space

Dedicated coworking areas equipped with desks, high-speed internet, and meeting rooms are available for residents. Perfect for video calls, focused work, or collaboration with other residents.
