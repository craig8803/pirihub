Frequently asked questions

Is WiFi reliable enough for remote work?

WiFi is available in all properties. For critical video calls or large uploads, we recommend confirming current speeds directly with Horayma before booking.

Is a car necessary?

For Casa Matutina, Atelier Aconchego, and Mini Casa Piri – no. All three are walkable to central Pirenópolis. For Casa Sol e Cachoeira, a car is necessary.

Are long stays possible?

Yes – please contact us directly for monthly rate options, which offer a significant discount over nightly rates.

What is the nearest airport?

Brasília International Airport (BSB) is approximately 150 km away. Goiânia Airport (GYN) is around 130 km. Both are connected by bus or transfer services. Horayma can arrange door-to-door transfers.

Do you offer early or late check-in?

Flexible check-in and check-out times are available on request, subject to availability. Contact Horayma directly when booking.
