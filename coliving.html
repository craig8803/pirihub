Coliving in Pirenópolis

Work remotely in paradise

Imagine starting your workday with a sunrise coffee overlooking mountains, taking lunch breaks by waterfalls, and ending your evening with new friends around a fire. At PiriHub, coliving means living and working in one of Brazil's most beautiful and inspiring settings.

Pirenópolis offers the perfect blend of tranquility and vibrant energy. This historic colonial town is surrounded by cerrado wilderness, cascading waterfalls, and dramatic landscapes. Yet it maintains a lively cultural scene with weekly markets, live music, artisan workshops, and a welcoming community of locals and international residents.

Surrounded by nature

Pirenópolis sits at the edge of Chapada dos Veadeiros, a region famous for its crystal-clear waterfalls, natural pools, and hiking trails. Within a 20-minute drive, you'll find dozens of waterfalls. Some are towering cascades, others are intimate swimming holes perfect for an afternoon dip. The cerrado landscape is alive with unique flora and fauna, dramatic rock formations, and endless opportunities for adventure.

After a day of work, sunset from the town's colonial stone bridge, weekend trips to nearby national parks, and the ever-present sound of flowing water create a lifestyle that's both productive and deeply connected to the natural world.

Vibrant yet relaxed

Pirenópolis has a rhythm all its own. Mornings are peaceful and productive. Afternoons invite exploration. Whether that's a swim under waterfalls, browsing artisan studios, or simply reading in a hammock. Evenings come alive with street food, live forró music, craft cocktails at local bars, and impromptu gatherings with your coliving community.

The town attracts a diverse crowd: Brazilian weekenders from Brasília, international travelers seeking authentic experiences, artists, musicians, and increasingly, remote workers discovering what it means to truly live where you love.
