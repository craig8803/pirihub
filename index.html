Welcoming spaces for coliving and remote work in Pirenópolis, Brazil. A beautiful colonial town surrounded by waterfalls, nature, and a vibrant community.

Work remotely in paradise

Imagine starting your workday with a sunrise coffee overlooking mountains, taking lunch breaks by waterfalls, and ending your evening with new friends around a fire. At PiriHub, coliving means living and working in one of Brazil's most beautiful and inspiring settings.

Pirenópolis offers the perfect blend of tranquility and vibrant energy. This historic colonial town is surrounded by cerrado wilderness, cascading waterfalls, and dramatic landscapes. Yet it maintains a lively cultural scene with weekly markets, live music, artisan workshops, and a welcoming community of locals and international residents.
