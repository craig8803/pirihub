Best For

Group retreats, team offsites, and coliving stays. The flagship property — the best-located house in the portfolio, with everything walkable and room for up to eight guests.

A beautifully restored colonial house in the heart of Pirenópolis — with a heated pool, jacuzzi, four bedrooms, gourmet grill kitchen, and everything walkable. The top choice for a group retreat or team offsite.

Built in the early 2000s in authentic colonial style — hand-crafted timber joinery without nails — Casa Matutina has been recently renovated and offers four fully air-conditioned bedrooms: one king, two queens, one double. Three bathrooms. The kitchen is comprehensively equipped: electric stove, microwave, two coffee makers, fridge, freezer, filtered water dispenser. Outside, the heated pool with jacuzzi and gourmet grill area with Alexa sound system make for exceptional evenings.

A two-car garage is included. The house is rented exclusively — no shared spaces with other guests.

Location

Steps from the Igreja Matriz — the town's 18th-century main church and the focal point of Pirenópolis — and within easy walking distance of every restaurant, bar, market, and cultural attraction in the historic center. You can leave the car in the garage for days.
