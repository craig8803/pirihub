Casa Matutina

The space

Welcome to Casa Matutina, a warm and character-filled house designed for people who work remotely and value both focus and quality of life. The property features four thoughtfully decorated bedrooms: one with a king-size bed, two with queen-size beds, and one with a double bed. Each room includes air conditioning, bedside lamps, and high-quality bed and bath linens for comfortable short or long stays.

The kitchen is fully equipped for everyday living and longer co-living stays, with an electric oven, microwave, sandwich maker, two coffee machines, refrigerator, freezer, and filtered hot and cold water—ideal for early work mornings, shared meals, and slow evenings.

Built in the 2000s in authentic colonial style, Casa Matutina blends craftsmanship and modern comfort. Its handcrafted wooden structure, assembled without nails, gives the house a calm, grounded atmosphere that many remote workers find ideal for concentration. The property has two shared bathrooms, an additional bathroom in the gourmet barbecue area, and a heated swimming pool with a hot tub—perfect for decompressing after a productive day.

Casa Matutina is designed for remote work. The house offers a fast, reliable internet connection and a variety of work-friendly environments. Guests can work at the large communal table in the living room, on the shaded verandah, at one of the outdoor tables, in one of the many hammocks across the property, or—if they prefer—right beside the pool. The flexibility of spaces supports different working styles, from deep-focus sessions to more relaxed, creative flows.

The gourmet barbecue kitchen and shared social areas encourage natural interaction, while still allowing plenty of room for privacy and personal rhythm—making Casa Matutina especially well suited to boutique co-living and longer nomad stays.

With a secure garage, generous common areas, and a shower that feels like a waterfall, the house balances practicality with comfort. The subtle scent of wood throughout creates a sense of calm that many guests associate with feeling settled rather than transient.

Guest access

Guests have access to all shared areas of Casa Matutina, including indoor and outdoor spaces designed for working, relaxing, and connecting with others.

Located in the historic center of Pirenópolis, the house is highly walkable. Cafés, bakeries, restaurants, small shops, and local attractions are all nearby, allowing you to structure your days without relying on a car—an important advantage for nomads staying mid- to long-term.

Other things to note

Casa Matutina is located just steps from the Matriz Church, one of Pirenópolis' most recognizable landmarks. Despite its central position, the house sits on a quiet street, offering a peaceful environment for work and rest.

From the property, guests can enjoy views of the surrounding mountains, including Morro do Frota, reinforcing the feeling of being connected to nature while staying in town. This balance of calm, community, and accessibility is what makes Casa Matutina especially appealing to digital nomads seeking a slower, more intentional base.

Whether you are staying for a few weeks or several months, Casa Matutina offers the infrastructure, atmosphere, and location to support both productive workdays and meaningful downtime. It's not just a place to stay—it's a place to settle in.
